Meet Sitara,
the protagonist of our parallel universe comic book series!
She was born on the morning of May 2nd, year 790060 in the Sombrero Galaxy. She liked reading books backwards, and talking in a laguage that she invented at the age of 4. She also loved wild animals!
Soon she discovered her superpowers of curiosity, empathy and the ability to make people laugh! She loved solving problems and developed a fascination for the stars!
As she explored her world, she fell in love with music, ninjutsu and physics! She also loved climbing trees and dancing on her terrace as she looked up at stars! She was obsessed with logical puzzles and philosphical conversations to feed her growing curiosity.....
