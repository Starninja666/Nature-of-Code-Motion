A voyage of a lifetime...
But HEY! What should she do next? She had so many interests and she loved it all! Her life until now was a random walk in a forest of tempting fruits and trees to climb!
Should she pick a single object of focus? Could she be a perfect piece in a jigsaw puzzle? Could she find the ONE THING to dedicate her life to? But maybe life is not so ideal...
With time, she experimented with a 100 different directions! She met people who inspired her, but importantly she realized life was not a jigsaw puzzle to solve, but a beautifully patterned fabric that needs to be stitched!
